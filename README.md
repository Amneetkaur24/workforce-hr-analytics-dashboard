# HR Analytics Dashboard | Tableau

## Project Overview

Organizations generate large volumes of workforce data, but transforming that data into meaningful insights for HR decision-making can be challenging.

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard built in Tableau** to analyze workforce composition, hiring trends, salary distribution, and employee demographics.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand workforce structure and explore employee-level insights through interactive filtering.

---

## Business Problem

HR teams often struggle to monitor workforce metrics such as employee growth, department distribution, demographic composition, and salary patterns. Without clear visualization, it becomes difficult to identify workforce trends and support strategic planning.

## Project Objective

The goal of this project was to develop an interactive Tableau dashboard that provides:

• Workforce overview metrics  
• Employee demographic insights  
• Salary distribution by education and age  
• Department-level employee distribution  
• Employee-level drill-down analysis  

---

## Dashboard Features

### Workforce Overview
Key workforce metrics displayed in the dashboard:

• Active Employees: **7,984**  
• Total Hired: **8,950**  
• Total Terminated: **966**

These KPIs help track workforce growth and attrition trends.

---

### Department Distribution

Largest departments within the organization:

1. Operations — **2,429 employees**
2. Sales — **1,634 employees**
3. Customer Service — **1,489 employees**

These departments represent the majority of the workforce.

---

### Workforce Demographics

Gender distribution:

• Male — **54%**  
• Female — **46%**

Age distribution indicates the highest concentration of employees in the **35–44 age group**, suggesting a mid-career workforce.

---

### Salary Analysis

Salary trends based on education level:

• High School — ~$63K  
• Bachelor — ~$66K – $74K  
• Master — ~$80K – $86K  
• PhD — ~$80K – $93K  

This demonstrates a positive relationship between education level and salary range.

---

### Employee Details Dashboard

The second dashboard provides detailed employee records including:

• Employee ID and name  
• Job role and department  
• Geographic location  
• Salary information  
• Hire date  
• Length of employment  

Interactive filters allow users to explore employee-level information based on demographics, role, and location.
